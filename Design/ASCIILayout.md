## Responsive Behavior Notes

- Mobile: Stack everything vertically
- Tablet: May keep some side-by-side elements
- Desktop: Full layouts with generous white space
- All breakpoints: Maintain book-like feel with clear section separation

## Visual Characteristics

- Double lines (â•) for page boundaries (conceptual)
- Rounded corners on all cards and containers
- Generous white space around all elements
- Subtle shadows for depth
- No harsh lines or borders
- Everything should feel soft and elegant
